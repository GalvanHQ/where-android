Introduction

This privacy policy ("Policy") describes how Ismam Hasan Ovi ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our mobile application Where (the "App").

By using the Where App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Policy.

2

Information We Collect

Personal Information
  • Account Information — Name, email address, and profile photo collected during registration.
  • Username — A unique @username that you choose for your account.
  • Bio — An optional short bio you can add to your profile.
Location Data
  • Live Location — Real-time GPS coordinates collected when you enable location sharing.
  • Sharing Settings — You choose which groups can see your location and for how long.
  • Location History — We do not store your location history after you stop sharing.
Device Information
  • Device ID — Device identifier used for push notifications.
  • FCM Token — Firebase Cloud Messaging token for delivering notifications.
Usage Data
  • App Interactions — Anonymous usage data collected to improve the app experience.
  • Crash Reports — Crash reports collected to identify and fix bugs.
