# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** May 6, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

This privacy policy ("Policy") describes how Ismam Hasan Ovi ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our mobile application "where" (the "App").

By using the where App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information:** When you register, we collect your name, email address, and profile photo.
- **Username:** A unique @username that you choose for your account.
- **Bio:** An optional short bio you can add to your profile.

### Location Data
- **Live Location:** When you enable location sharing, we collect your real-time GPS coordinates.
- **Sharing Settings:** You choose which groups can see your location and for how long.
- **Location History:** We do not store your location history after you stop sharing.

### Device Information
- **Device ID:** We collect your device identifier for push notifications.
- **FCM Token:** Firebase Cloud Messaging token for sending notifications.

### Usage Data
- **App Interactions:** We collect anonymous usage data to improve the app.
- **Crash Reports:** We collect crash reports to fix bugs.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

| Purpose | Information Used |
|--------|-----------------|
| Provide location sharing | Location, Group membership |
| Send notifications | FCM Token, Device ID |
| Manage your account | Name, Email, Profile photo |
| Improve the app | Anonymous usage data |

## 4. Information Sharing

### We **DO NOT** sell your personal information.

### We share information only with:

| Recipient | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| Firebase | Authentication, Database, Push notifications |
| Group members | Location (only when you share) |
| Cloud Run | Chat server (real-time messaging) |

### Location Sharing
- Your location is **only shared** with members of groups you have joined.
- You control when to start/stop sharing.
- Your location is never shared publicly.
